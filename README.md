## 🧩 What this app does

wp-ai-schema-generator is a WordPress plugin that helps you add JSON-LD schema markup to your site.

It reads your content and tries to detect the page type for you. Then it builds schema for the right content format.

It supports common schema types like:

- Article
- FAQ
- HowTo
- Product
- Recipe
- LocalBusiness
- Course
- Event

It also works with more content types and can use more than one AI provider.

## 🧠 How it works

The plugin checks your page content and looks for signals that show what type of page it is.

For example:

- A blog post may become Article schema
- A recipe page may become Recipe schema
- A product page may become Product schema
- A business page may become LocalBusiness schema
- A guide with steps may become HowTo schema

This helps search engines understand your page content in a clearer way.
